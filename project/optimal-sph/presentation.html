Motivation

Lagrangian Hydrodynamics

  • System State \(\vec{x}_1,\dots,\vec{x}_N\) and \(\vec{v}_1,\dots,\vec{v}_N\)
  • Constants: \(\vec{g}, m, \nu, N\)
  • Decision variables: \(p_1,\dots, p_N\)
  • Governing equations:

\(\ddot{\vec{x}}_i = \vec{g}\) \(+\nu \nabla^2 \vec{v}_i\) \(\underbrace{- \frac{1}{\rho_i}\nabla p_i}_{\text{Pressure Acceleration}}\)

subject to \(\frac{D\rho_i(\vec{x}_1,\dots,\vec{x}_N)}{Dt} = 0\)

Discretization

  • Governing equations:

\(\ddot{\vec{x}}_i = \vec{g}+\nu \nabla^2 \vec{v}_i- \frac{1}{\rho_i}\nabla p_i\)

\(\frac{D\rho_i(\vec{x}_1,\dots,\vec{x}_N)}{Dt} = 0\)

Discretization

  • Governing equations:

\(\ddot{\vec{x}}_i = \vec{g}+ \langle\nu \nabla^2 \vec{v}_i\rangle- \frac{1}{\langle\rho_i\rangle}\langle\nabla p_i\rangle\)

\(\left\langle\rho_i\left(\begin{array}{c} \vec{x}_1(t+\Delta t)\\\dots\\\vec{x}_N(t+\Delta t) \end{array}\right)\right\rangle = \rho_0\)

Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics

\(\begin{align} \langle f_i\rangle &= \sum_{j \in \mathcal{N}_i} \frac{m}{\rho_j} \, f_j \, W_{ij}\qquad \langle\nabla f_i\rangle = \rho_i \sum_{j \in \mathcal{N}_i} m \, \left( \frac{f_i}{\rho_i^2} + \frac{f_j}{\rho_j^2} \right) \, \nabla W_{ij}\qquad \langle \nabla \cdot \vec{f}_i \rangle = -\frac{1}{\rho_i} \sum_{j \in \mathcal{N}_i} m \left(\vec{f}_i - \vec{f}_j\right) \cdot \nabla W_{ij} \end{align}\)

  • 2D Gaussian Kernel1 such as \(W_{ij} := W(\vec{x}_i - \vec{x}_j, \hbar) = \frac{16}{\pi\hbar^2}\cdot \max\left(0,1-\frac{\left|\vec{x}_i-\vec{x}_j\right|}{\hbar}\right)^3 - 4\cdot \max\left(0,\frac{1}{2} - \frac{\left|\vec{x}_i-\vec{x}_j\right|}{\hbar}\right)^3\)

\(\hbar\)

What we need to solve

  • Newtons Equations of Motion (split and discretized)1

\(\begin{align}\vec{v}^*_i(t)&=\vec{v}_i(t) +\Delta t \cdot \left(\vec{g} + \vec{a}^{vis}_i(t)\right)\\\vec{v}_i(t+\Delta t)&=\vec{v}^*_i(t) +\Delta t \cdot \vec{a}^p_i(t)\\ \vec{x}_i(t+\Delta t)&=\vec{x}_i(t) +\Delta t \cdot \vec{v}_i(t+\Delta t)\end{align}\)

  • Pressures are a constraint force2

Find \(p_i\) such that \(\vec{a}^{p}_i = -\frac{1}{\rho_i}\nabla p_i\) leads to \(\rho_i(t+\Delta t) = \rho_0\)

Particle Deficiency

Find \(p_i\) such that \(\vec{a}^{p}_i = -\frac{1}{\rho_i}\nabla p_i\) leads to \(\rho_i(t+\Delta t) = \rho_0\)

  • Positivity constraint \(p_i \geq 0\)

  • Incompressibility constraint \(\rho_i(t+\Delta t) \leq \rho_0\)

Linearizing the constraint

  • Incompressibility constraint \(\rho_i(t+\Delta t) \leq \rho_0\)

\(\rho_i(t+\Delta t) \approx \rho_i(t) + \frac{D\rho_i}{D t} \Delta t\)

\(\rho_i^*(t) \quad\quad\,\,= \rho_i(t) - \rho_i(t) \nabla \cdot(\vec{v}^*_i + \Delta t \vec{a}^p_i) \Delta t\)

  • Linearized Incompressibility Constraint \(\rho_i^*(t) - \rho_0 \leq \epsilon\)

Formulating an Optimization Problem

  • Linearized Incompressibility Constraint \(\rho_i^*(t) - \rho_0 \leq \epsilon\)
  • Positivity constraint \(p_i \geq 0\)
  • \(\Longrightarrow\) Explosions are feasible

Formulating an Optimization Problem

  • Idea: Minimize Action

\(\begin{align}S(\vec{p}) &= \frac{1}{2}\sum_i \left|\left|\, \vec{a}^p_i \,\right|\right|_2^2\\ &\propto \sum_i \langle\nabla p_i\rangle \cdot \langle\nabla p_i\rangle \\S_i (\vec{p})&:= \left(\rho_i \sum_{j \in \mathcal{N}_i} m_j \, \left( \frac{p_i}{\rho_i^2} + \frac{p_j}{\rho_j^2} \right) \, \nabla W_{ij}\right)^2\end{align}\)

  • Gauss’ principle: minimize Action subject to constraints.1

  1. PMPG: Alhussein & Daqaq 2024

Formulating an Optimization Problem

\(\begin{align} &\min_{\vec{p}\in ℝ^N} &\frac{1}{2}\sum_i S_i(\vec{p}) &\\ &\text{s.t.}&\forall i\in\{1,\dots,N\}: &\quad p_i \geq 0 \\ &&\forall i\in\{1,\dots,N\}: &\quad \langle\rho^*_i\rangle - \rho_0 \leq \epsilon \\ \end{align}\)

  • This is a convex Quadratic Program with a Least Squares objective
    • \(S_i(\vec{p})\) is quadratic in \(\vec{p}\) with positive semidefinite Hessian
    • \(p_i\) is linear in \(\vec{p}\)
    • \(\langle\rho^*_i\rangle - \rho_0\) is affine in \(\vec{p}\)
